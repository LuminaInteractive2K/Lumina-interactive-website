Quantum Particle Symphony Help

About

Quantum Particle Symphony is a cutting-edge interactive experience where you conduct a symphony of quantum-inspired particles using gestures. Explore complex emergent behaviors and create mesmerizing audiovisual compositions.

Controls

  • Particle Count: Adjusts the number of particles in the simulation.
  • Interaction Strength: Controls the intensity of forces between particles.
  • System Energy: Influences the overall kinetic energy and activity level of the particles.

Physics Parameters

Fine-tune the underlying physics simulation:

  • Gravity: Strength of the global gravitational pull.
  • Viscosity: Resistance to motion within the system.
  • Entanglement Prob.: Likelihood of particles becoming quantumly entangled.
  • Superposition Decay: Rate at which particles collapse from superposition states.

Input Methods

Interact with the particle system using:

  • Gesture Control: Requires a Leap Motion controller for precise hand tracking.
  • Camera Input: Uses your webcam for basic motion detection.
  • Mouse/Touch: Control particle flow with your cursor or touch input.

Use the simulation mode if no input device is connected.
